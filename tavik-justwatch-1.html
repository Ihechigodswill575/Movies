Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to TAVIK JUSTWATCH ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit reviews or ratings on our platform.

  • Movie ratings and reviews you submit
  • Search queries you enter on the site
  • Usage data collected through Google Analytics and Google AdSense

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small data files placed on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Saving your movie ratings and reviews locally
  • Google Analytics to understand site traffic
  • Google AdSense for advertising purposes

Third Party Services

We use The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie and TV show information. TMDB has its own privacy policy available at themoviedb.org. Movie data and images are provided by TMDB.

Data Storage

Your ratings and reviews are stored locally in your browser's storage. We do not store personal data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
